This Power BI HR Analytics dashboard includes several DAX measures to track and evaluate employee attrition, tenure trends and workforce risk indicators. Below is an overview of the main KPIs implemented:

üìä Attrition & Workforce Metrics

KPI	Description

Attrition (Leavers)	Calculates the total number of employees who have left the organisation within the selected time period.

Total Attrition Number	Total count of departures, used as a base for further calculations.

Overall Attrition Rate %	Percentage of employees leaving, based on total headcount.

Overtime Attrition Rate (Work-Life Balance)	Attrition rate specifically for employees with high overtime‚Äîused to indicate poor work-life balance.

Total Head Count	Total number of employees currently in the organisation.

üë§ Tenure & Risk Analysis

KPI	Description

Average Tenure	Average duration of employment for current staff.

High Risk Target	Defines a risk threshold to identify employees who are more likely to leave based on specific conditions (e.g., dissatisfaction, workload).

High Risk Tenure Attrition (< 2 years)	Measures the attrition rate among employees with a tenure of less than 2 years‚Äîearly leavers.
